Welcome to my PIXI projects page!

Here you'll find a collection of my personal, experimental work.

These projects are a result of me adapting some of my earlier p5.js creations and leveraging the power of Pixi.js to develop my skills further.

I've prefixed these sections with 'pixi' as they were built using Pixi.js, which is a rendering engine that allows for the creation of interactive graphic experiences, cross-platform applications, and games.

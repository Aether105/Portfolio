Welcome to my p5 projects page!

Here you will find a variety of personal, experimental projects that I have been developing to grow my skills.

Some of my early experimentation has been created whilst working through the "Nature of Code" by Daniel Shiffman.

I have prefixed these sections with 'p5' as these projects have utilised p5.js, which is a JavaScript library that includes a number of tools, that make it easy to experiment with dynamic and physics-based interaction.
